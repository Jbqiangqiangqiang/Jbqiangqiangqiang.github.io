Welcome

Hi! Welcome to my homepage. I’m Yangbangyan Jiang (姜阳邦彦), a Ph.D. student in Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS), jointly supervised by Xiaochun Cao (Professor at IIE, CAS) and Qingming Huang (Professor at UCAS, IEEE Fellow). My research interests include machine learning and deep learning. I am also proud to have collaborated with Qianqian Xu (Professor at ICT, CAS), Zhiyong Yang (Ph.D. Student at IIE, CAS) and Ke Ma (Ph.D. Student at IIE, CAS).
